How to scan files

First, click the "Scan Documents" button on the left hand side column.

You will then be given two options. You can use AI to sort your files or sort them manually. To use the first method, click the "Choose Files" button on the left hand side and select the file(s) you want to upload. Then select whether each document has its own file or there are multiple documents per file.

What this means is that there are two ways to use the AI. You can scan each patient's document into its own file; eg Briankelleher.pdf or scan multiple into one file; eg patients.pdf. The AI will automatically sort the larger file into smaller ones if you select the multiple document option. You can upload multiple files at once.

Then select what kind of template you would like to select. Currently, only the SSC X-RAY/CT option works, but the other options are being developed.

Once you have selected all of the options, select the "Upload" button to put them into the database.

The second way is by uploading individual files manually. To use this method, click on the "Choose Files" button on the right hand side and select the file you want to upload. Enter the name you want to give the report and the date you want to give the report. Select a patient's chart to upload the document to using the checkbox. Click the upload button to enter the information into the patient's chart.

How to view files

Click on the "View Documents" button on the left hand side of the screen.

Search your patient using the search bar and once found, click on the checkbox for the relevant patient and then click the "Search" button.

Here, you can view the PDF and the report title and date. You can also download the PDF for distribution or printing purposes.
